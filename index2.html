ABOUT MLC

At MLC, our goal is to provide learners with practical and high – quality education to equip them to handle and solve real – world complex problems through informed research.

WHY MLC

A common cliché in statistical analysis is “Garbage in, garbage out”.

Therefore, at MLC, we don’t just teach how to produce software outputs but more importantly how to understand and interpret the results using appropriate statistical techniques. Our courses are designed to meet the growing demands of today’s researchers and professionals.
MLC will strengthen your research and data analysis skills.
MLC will help you to explore courses that teach you the practical and fundamentals of research and data analysis.
MLC offer you the unique opportunity to unlock actionable insights from data that make sense using:
  • R programming language
  • STATA
  • Statistical Package for Social Sciences (SPSS)
  • Excel
  • ChatGPT
  • Kobo Toolbox
  • Open Data Kit (ODK) collect
  • Statistics for Research
  • AI tools for Literature Search

Meet Your Instructor

Eric Asiamah Yeboah is a researcher and data analyst with a Master’s degree in Agricultural Economics from the University of Reading, UK. He also holds a certificate in Reproducible Research Fundamentals from the World Bank’s Development Impact Evaluation (DIME) Research Group. He has participated in several field surveys including those organized by Africa Rice, International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA) and Crops Research Institute. Over the course of his academic and professional career, he has built and developed a skill set in data analysis and statistics and his goal is to use his experience in data science and research to help other people learn how to analyze data, gain fundamental knowledge in statistics and skills required to carry out reproducible research.
