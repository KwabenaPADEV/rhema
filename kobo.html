Course Title: Online Data Collection using Kobo Toolboox

Course Description: Data is the driving force across the various sectors of our economy, including government, health, academia, agriculture, industry etc. for making informed decisions in today’s world. One major factor for this development is the expansion in the digital landscape that has led to volumes of data been generated. The emergence of online data collection tools has altered the method for data gathering, providing efficiency, accessibility and scalability. It is therefore imperative for individuals and organisations to be equipped with the methods and tools that make it possible to gather data in virtual environment. This course is designed to assist participants to gain the requisite skills and knowledge to effectively handle real-world examples showcasing successful data collection using online data collection tools such as Kobo Toolbox, and manage the data for various purposes for achieving specific goals.

Course Description: This short course aims to equip learners with in – depth understanding of the Kobo Toolbox and ethical considerations and best practices for online data collection. Participants will be provided with hands – on practical sessions on the application of Kobo Toolbox for online data collection tools.

By the end of this course, participants are expected to:

  1. Gain proficiency about key concepts about exploring the core features and functionalities of Kobo Toolbox
  2. Understand the principles of designing user-friendly and effective data collection forms
  3. Know how to apply form validation rules to ensure accurate and complete data
  4. Gain knowledge about determining the appropriate deployment methods: Online vs. offline data collection

Course content:

Lesson 1
  • Introduction to Kobo Toolbox interface as a powerful online data tool
    • Exploring the key features of Kobo Toolbox and creating account
Lesson 2
  • Choosing the appropriate deployment methods: Online vs. offline data collection
    • How the forms can be tailored for various devices: Mobile phones, tablets, and web browsers
Lesson 3
  • Understanding the data export options: Generating reports and visualizations
    • Learning from real-world case studies: Success stories and challenges.

Course Prerequisites:

  • Basic computer literacy and familiarity with internet usage.
  • No prior experience with online data collection tools , as the course covers fundamentals.
  • A genuine interest in data analysis.
